# Exercise 1: Understanding AI Types

## Problem
Classify the following scenarios as either Narrow AI or General AI:

1. A chess-playing computer (Deep Blue) that beat Garry Kasparov
2. A hypothetical AI that can do any intellectual task humans can do
3. A spam email filter
4. An autonomous vehicle that can drive in any condition
5. A medical diagnosis system trained on specific diseases
6. A personal assistant that can help with any task
7. A recommendation system on Netflix
8. An AI that can learn new skills in new domains without retraining

## Questions

1. **What is the key difference** between your Narrow AI and General AI classifications?

2. **Which capabilities** would make an AI system classified as General AI?

3. **Why is General AI still theoretical** while Narrow AI exists today?

4. **Can a Narrow AI system ever become General AI?** Explain your reasoning.

5. **What challenges** prevent us from building General AI today?

## Follow-up

For each Narrow AI example above:
- Describe what data it uses
- Explain what task it performs
- Discuss how you would measure its success
